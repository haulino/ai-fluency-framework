# Discernment Competency
The ability to critically evualte what AI produces, how it produces it and how it behaves. A quality control system for AI collaboration.

Requirements
- Domain expertise. Knowing enough to judge quality.
- Understading how AI systems work and their typical shortcomings.

Breakdown
- Product Discernment: Is the output factually accurate? Audience appropiate? Coherent and well-structured? Does it meet requirements and add value?
- Process Discernment:  How the AI arrived at its output, looking for logical errors, attention gaps, or inappropriate reasoning
- Performance Discernment: How the AI behaves within the interaction process itself, considering whether its communication style is effective for your needs
